Dumps

Now, let's talk dumps

"Dumps" refer to stolen information from the magnetic stripe of credit or debit cards. This data includes details such as the cardholder's name, card number, expiration date and pin. Individuals who engage in unauthorized activities may obtain these dumps and use them to create fake cards. It's important to understand that this process involves stealing someone's card details, which is illegal and against the rules.
